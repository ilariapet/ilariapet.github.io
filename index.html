About Me

After a first experience in the healthcare sector, I got a Postgraduate Master in Data Science, completing a thesis focused on Machine Learning. Since 2020, I have worked on a variety of projects in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Natural Language Processing applied to Information Retrieval systems. My work has focused in particular on integrating Learning to Rank algorithms and Search Quality Evaluation frameworks within e-commerce platforms, with the goal of improving both performance and the relevance of search results. More recently, I have been involved in projects exploring the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) into search engines, aiming to enhance user experience through semantic understanding and generative capabilities. I also provide consulting services on Apache Solr and Elasticsearch, supporting organizations in optimizing their search infrastructure. Additionally, I am an active member of the Information Retrieval research community: I contribute to open source projects, share insights through blog posts and technical talks, and regularly participate in international conferences such as Berlin Buzzwords.
